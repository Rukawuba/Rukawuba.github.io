Books

Reading has always been a big hobby of mine ,but recently it has become of my favorite things do. So I figured I would list some of my favorite books that I've read this year. I hope this gives you the inspiration to pick up one of these books or just pick up any book in general.

Discuss a book with me!
Rich Dad Poor Dad

Robert Kiyosaki's story about how he learned from his two fathers. His real father who had the mindset of a poor man and then the father of his best friend, who had the mindset of a rich man. Great place to start if you want to improve your financial literacy.

The Reluctant Fundamentals

Recommended by a great friend of mine, and turned out to be a very interesting read. Discusses a man finding his identity in America throughout his college years as an immigrant.

The Way of the Superior Man

A book that looks to challenge men and how they interact with their surroundings.
